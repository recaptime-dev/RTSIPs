# RTSIP Editors

The RTSIP Editors are responsible for reviewing proposals received from both team members and the community for inclusion in the RTSIP repository.

## Responsibilities

Responsibilities include:

* Reviewing proposals received from both team members and the community for inclusion in the RTSIP repository following both the RTSIP process and style guide
* Moderating the RTSIP discussions in the issue tracker and merge requests following the Community Code of Conduct
* Creating and managing tracking issues in the issue tracker for each proposal filed
